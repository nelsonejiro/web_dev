Meet Our Chefs

Each of our chefs brings the spirit of their homeland into every meal. From traditional family recipes to years of regional mastery, their hands carry stories from across the globe.

Chef Mei Lin

Sichuan, China

Master of bold spices and delicate dumplings. Trained in Chengdu, passionate about balance and tradition.

Chef Adeolu A.

Lagos, Nigeria

Guardian of Yoruba heritage, his Ayamase sauce is both fiery and unforgettable.

Chef Nneka Okoro

Enugu, Nigeria

Specialist in white soup and native spices. Every pot tells an Igbo tale of warmth and wellness.

Chef Umar Bello

Kano, Nigeria

Master of Tuwo Shinkafa and ancient northern spices. His dishes are hearty, bold, and deeply rooted.

Chef Amélie Rousseau

Lyon, France

Elegant and passionate. Her Coq au Vin brings old-world comfort and finesse to your table.

Chef Lukas Stein

Munich, Germany

From beer halls to your plate — his Schweinshaxe is a rustic celebration of Bavarian soul food.

Chef Amina Z.

Marrakech / Berlin

Curator of the Unity Platter — blending borders, spices, and love into one unforgettable dish.
